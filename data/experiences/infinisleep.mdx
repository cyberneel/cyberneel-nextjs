A smart alarm and sleep experience for the InfiniTime smartwatch OS.

=ReAdMoRe=

## Highlights
- Developed a smart alarm for InfiniTime OS, improving the wake-up experience through user-centric design.
- Implemented an nRF SDK motor PWM library to precisely control alarm vibration strength and intervals.
- Leveraged LVGL and FreeRTOS to build the embedded application, ensuring efficient resource management.
- Integrated Apple's ANCS specification over BLE to enable seamless iOS notifications on the smartwatch.
- Actively worked with the PineTime community and opened PRs with testing builds of the firmware.
